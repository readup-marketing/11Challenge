Chapter One: The Letter

"Who's calling you back?"

"The Circle." Gran's hands tightened on the steering wheel. "That's all you need to know for now."

As the sun began to set, Thornwood Manor finally appeared. It rose from the hillside like a dark giant. It was bigger than Elara had imagined—huge and made of grey stone that looked almost black in the fading light. Towers reached toward the darkening sky. The windows looked like dark eyes watching them approach. Strange stone creatures sat along the roof edges, and ivy climbed up the walls like reaching hands.

When they got out of the car, the enormous wooden doors swung open by themselves. A tall, thin man in a perfect black suit stood in the doorway.
